# Form 720 PCORI Fee - 29 Covered Lives

Prepared using IRS Form 720 (Rev. June 2026) for a company with 29 average covered lives.

Assumption used for the draft PDF:
- Applicable self-insured health plan.
- Plan year ended on or after October 1, 2025, and before October 1, 2026.
- For a typical calendar-year plan ending December 31, 2025, the applicable rate is $3.84.

Calculation:
- 29 covered lives x $3.84 = $111.36.

Draft PDF entries:
- Quarter ending: 06/30/2026.
- Part II, Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Fee, IRS No. 133, applicable self-insured health plans:
  - Row (d): 29 average covered lives.
  - Fee: $111.36.
  - Tax for IRS No. 133: $111.36.
- Part II, line 2 total: $111.36.
- Part III, line 3 total tax: $111.36.
- Part III, line 10 balance due: $111.36.

Before filing, complete:
- Company name, address, and EIN.
- Signature, printed name, date, title, and telephone number.
- Paid preparer section, if applicable.
- Third party designee section, if desired.

Important alternate rate:
- If the plan year ended before October 1, 2025, use the $3.47 rate instead.
- 29 covered lives x $3.47 = $100.63.

Filing note:
- The PCORI fee is reported annually on Form 720 and is due July 31 of the year following the last day of the plan year.
- For EFTPS, IRS guidance says to apply the payment to the second quarter.
